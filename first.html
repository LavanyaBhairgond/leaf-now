About Us

Just as people are waking up to the stark, ever-growing realities of climate change and its impact, more people should also be made aware of their participatory role in combatting climate change. It is often said that the “best way to learn is to do” and indeed, there is perhaps a no better way to get started on a journey towards offsetting that carbon footprint than to encourage people to plant more trees themselves, rather than wait for third parties to step up. Planting trees is one of the easiest and most sustainable ways to affect the environment positively, and instituting that as a reward is sure to mobilize the workforce towards the planet’s greater good.
